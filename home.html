What We Can Do

Three ways to act today

Climate action doesn't require grand gestures. Millions of small, consistent choices add up to systemic change. Here's where to start — as a student, a citizen, and a consumer.

01
♻️

Reduce Waste

Household waste produces methane as it decomposes in landfill — a greenhouse gas 80× more potent than CO₂ over 20 years. Rethinking consumption is one of the fastest wins.

  • Carry a reusable bag and water bottle daily
  • Compost food scraps rather than binning them
  • Choose products with minimal or recyclable packaging
02
⚡

Save Energy

Energy use in homes and transport accounts for nearly 40% of global CO₂ emissions. Cutting consumption at home is one of the most direct levers individuals hold.

  • Switch to LED bulbs and smart power strips
  • Unplug chargers and devices on standby
  • Walk, cycle, or use public transport where possible
03
🌱

Plant Trees

Reforestation is among the most effective natural strategies for carbon sequestration. A single mature tree absorbs around 22 kg of CO₂ per year and supports biodiversity.

  • Join or fund a local tree-planting initiative
  • Support verified reforestation charities
  • Grow a food garden or houseplants at home
